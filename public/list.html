What is Snowball?

Snowball is a crowd-marketing platform.

You have a creative project. We get your fans to spread the word. Snowball is a new way to promote your creative project and hook your fans up with unique benefits.


How it Works

  • 1. Post a Project

    Create your own optimized landing page in less than five minutes - it's free.

  • 2. Incentizive Sharing

    Reward your fans for sharing your project. Cap the number of rewards given to create scarcity value.

  • 3. Build Awareness

    Build buzz as fans share your project on Facebook and Twitter.

What can you do with Snowball?


Publish your eBook.

eBook authors use Snowball to generate buzz when a new book is published. Most authors reward their loyal fans with free copies of their work.




Launch your Startup.

Entrepreneurs big and small use Snowball to generate awareness about their business by incentivizing their loyal early adapters to spread the word.




Fill your Class.

Instructors from yoga to karate use Snowball to promote their classes and offer discount coupons to fans who spread the word.




Sellout your Event.

Conference organizers offer special discounts to fans who evangelize their event on Facebook or Twitter.




Attract Local Foodies.

Tacos anyone? Food Trucks use Snowball to inform locals when they're near with the food their fans love.
